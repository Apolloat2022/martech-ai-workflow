# Market Claims Taxonomy — US (fictional, illustrative)

- "Clinically tested" and "clinically proven" are both high-scrutiny; a named
  study reference is required for either phrase in this market.
- "#1" and "best-selling" claims require a named, dated, third-party source
  and must specify the category the ranking applies to.
- "Guaranteed" claims must state the actual guarantee terms in the same
  creative unit, not merely reference a policy page.
