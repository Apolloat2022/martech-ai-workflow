# Market Claims Taxonomy — EU (fictional, illustrative)

- Comparative claims ("better than X") require the comparison basis to be stated
  in the same creative, not in a footnote on a separate page.
- "Clinically tested" is permitted only with a named study reference visible on
  request; "clinically proven" is treated as a stricter claim requiring
  regulatory-grade evidence and is high-scrutiny in this market.
- Environmental claims ("clean", "natural") require a named standard or
  certification body — a general impression is not sufficient here.
