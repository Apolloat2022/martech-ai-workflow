# Meridian Retail Group — Brand Voice & Tone

MRG creative speaks with quiet confidence, never hype. Preferred register: specific,
sensory, and grounded in what a customer can actually notice. Avoid absolute language
("the best", "the only") in favor of comparative, attributable claims ("customers in
our 2025 panel reported...").

Tone words: warm, precise, unhurried. Avoid: urgent, exclusive, limited-time pressure
language outside genuinely time-bound offers.
