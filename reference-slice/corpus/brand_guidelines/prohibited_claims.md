# Prohibited & High-Scrutiny Claim Patterns

The following claim patterns require a substantiation source before they can ship in
any market. Creative using these patterns without a linked substantiation record is
routed to Legal & Compliance review, not cleared automatically.

- "clinically proven" / "clinically tested" — requires a named study, sample size, and duration
- "guaranteed" / "guarantee results" — requires the actual guarantee terms
- "#1" / "best-selling" / "top-rated" — requires a named, dated, third-party source
- "cures" / "eliminates" / "permanently removes" — requires regulatory-grade substantiation; these are high-risk in every market
- "clean" / "natural" / "chemical-free" — requires a defined standard the product meets, not a vibe
